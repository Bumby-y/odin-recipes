Taco Salad with Lime Vinegar Dressing

Description

Taco Salad with Lime Vinegar Dressing is a fresh, vibrant, and satisfying meal that combines seasoned ground beef, crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, black beans, crunchy tortilla chips, and a zesty homemade lime vinegar dressing. It's perfect for a quick lunch or a light dinner, offering all the flavors of tacos in a refreshing salad form.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup tortilla chips, slightly crushed
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
  • Lime Vinegar Dressing:
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
    • Juice of 2 limes
    • 1 teaspoon honey
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef or turkey until browned. Drain excess fat, add taco seasoning and a splash of water, and simmer for 2-3 minutes. Set aside to cool slightly.
  2. In a small bowl or jar, whisk together olive oil, white wine vinegar, lime juice, honey, minced garlic, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. In a large salad bowl, combine chopped lettuce, cherry tomatoes, black beans, shredded cheese, black olives, red onion, and avocado.
  4. Add the cooked taco meat to the salad bowl and toss gently to combine.
  5. Drizzle the lime vinegar dressing over the salad and toss again until everything is evenly coated.
  6. Top with crushed tortilla chips and fresh cilantro, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!
