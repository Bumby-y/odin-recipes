Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic
  3. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer 10 minutes
  4. Mix ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan, and egg in a bowl
  5. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions
  6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a 9x13 baking dish
  7. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and cheese
  8. Bake covered for 25 minutes, uncovered for 10 minutes
  9. Let cool 5 minutes before serving
